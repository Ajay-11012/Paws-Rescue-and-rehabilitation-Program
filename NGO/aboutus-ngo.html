About Project Paws

Project Paws is a dedicated animal rescue and rehabilitation platform designed to help injured, sick, and homeless street animals. Our platform connects volunteers, NGOs, and rescue teams to provide timely medical care, safe shelters, and adoption services. By reporting injured animals and collaborating with our team, users play an active role in giving street animals a second chance at life.

Our mission is to create a safe and compassionate environment for all street animals. We strive to reduce animal suffering by ensuring quick rescue responses, proper medical treatment, and long-term care. Through awareness campaigns, community support, and active volunteer engagement, we aim to build a society that values and protects every animal.
