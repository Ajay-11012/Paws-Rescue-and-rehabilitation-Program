About Project Paws

Project Paws is an initiative dedicated to protecting and supporting injured, abandoned, and homeless street animals. Many animals on the streets suffer from injuries, diseases, and lack of shelter, often without receiving the help they need. Our platform was created to bridge the gap between people who want to help and organizations that can provide rescue and medical care.

The platform allows users to easily report animals that are injured, sick, or in danger. Once a report is submitted, nearby volunteers, rescue teams, and NGOs are notified so they can respond quickly. This system helps reduce rescue time and ensures animals receive timely treatment and protection.

In addition to rescue operations, Project Paws also focuses on rehabilitation and adoption programs. Animals that recover from treatment are given the opportunity to find safe and loving homes, allowing them to live better lives.

Our platform also promotes awareness about animal welfare and encourages communities to participate in protecting street animals. Through technology, collaboration, and compassion, we aim to create a supportive ecosystem where every animal receives the care and respect it deserves.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide immediate rescue, medical care, and rehabilitation for injured and vulnerable street animals. We aim to create a strong network of volunteers, NGOs, and veterinary professionals who work together to ensure animals receive the help they need.

We strive to build an efficient digital platform where citizens can report animal emergencies quickly and rescue teams can respond without delay. By combining technology with compassion, we aim to make rescue operations faster, more organized, and more effective.

Our mission also includes promoting responsible animal care within communities. Through awareness, volunteer participation, and adoption programs, we aim to reduce animal suffering and provide safer lives for street animals.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a world where no animal is left helpless on the streets. We envision a future where communities actively participate in animal welfare and every rescued animal receives proper care, protection, and a chance to live in a loving home.

Our Core Values
