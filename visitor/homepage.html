About the Organization

Our organization is dedicated to protecting and rescuing animals in need. We focus on helping injured, abandoned, and stray animals by connecting compassionate citizens with rescue teams, volunteers, and NGOs.

Through this platform, users can easily report animals requiring help. Once reported, nearby volunteers and organizations are notified so they can provide medical care, shelter, and rehabilitation.

We believe technology can play a powerful role in animal welfare. By creating a digital rescue network, we aim to make the rescue process faster, more organized, and transparent for everyone involved.

Our mission is to build a caring community where humans and animals coexist with compassion, respect, and responsibility.
